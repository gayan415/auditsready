# LAWYER INTERVIEW QUESTIONNAIRE
## AuditsReady Legal Counsel Selection

**Purpose:** Use this questionnaire when interviewing potential lawyers to review your legal documents and provide ongoing counsel.

**Goal:** Find a qualified SaaS/technology lawyer with GDPR and AI liability experience

---

## BEFORE THE CALL

**Send them:**
- [ ] This entire LAWYER_REVIEW_NEEDED folder (6 documents)
- [ ] Link to your website: https://auditsready.com
- [ ] Brief business description (see "Email to Lawyer" template)

**Ask them to:**
- [ ] Review the LAWYER-REVIEW-SUMMARY.md document (10-15 min read)
- [ ] Provide hourly rate and estimated total cost

### Deal-Breakers (Your Evaluation)
**Red flags to watch for:**
- ❌ No SaaS experience
- ❌ No GDPR experience (critical for worldwide service)
- ❌ Quotes <$2,000 (likely insufficient scope) or >$20,000 (overpriced for stage)
- ❌ Can't start within 2 weeks
- ❌ Won't provide fixed fee or estimate
- ❌ Dismisses AI liability concerns
- ❌ Doesn't ask clarifying questions about the business
