## 1. PURPOSE

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines prohibited uses of AuditsReady's Services. This AUP is part of our Terms of Service and applies to all customers.

Violation of this AUP may result in termination of your project, refusal of future services, with or without notice.

**Note:** Services are provided via email-based consulting. You are responsible for proper use of our deliverables and maintaining confidentiality.

## 2. PERMITTED USES

You may use AuditsReady's Services to:

- ✅ Analyze your organization's documentation for ISO 9001 compliance gaps
- ✅ Receive recommendations for improving Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)
- ✅ Prepare for ISO 9001 audits and certifications
- ✅ Obtain guidance on quality management system documentation
- ✅ Implement recommendations provided in gap analysis reports
- ✅ Share deliverables with your internal team members within your organization

## 3. PROHIBITED USES

### 3.1 Illegal Activities
You may NOT use the Services to:

- ❌ Violate any applicable laws, regulations, or industry standards
- ❌ Infringe intellectual property rights of others
- ❌ Engage in fraud, misrepresentation, or deceptive practices
- ❌ Launder money or engage in financial crimes
- ❌ Violate export control or sanctions laws

### 3.2 Abuse of Services
You may NOT:

- ❌ **Resell or redistribute** our deliverables (gap analysis reports, recommendations) to third parties
- ❌ **Offer our reports as your own work** to other businesses
- ❌ **Submit documents you don't own** or have authorization to share with us
- ❌ **Request multiple projects under different names** to abuse pricing or guarantees
- ❌ **Use our analysis services for competitors** or on behalf of competing businesses

### 3.3 Competitive Use
You may NOT:

- ❌ Use our deliverables to **build a competing ISO consulting business**
- ❌ Reverse-engineer our AI analysis methodologies
- ❌ Copy our report formats, templates, or proprietary analysis frameworks
- ❌ Use our services to **provide consulting to your own clients** (reselling prohibited)
- ❌ Share our deliverables with other ISO consultants or competing firms

### 3.4 Harmful Content
You may NOT submit via email:

- ❌ **Malware, viruses, or malicious code** in attachments
- ❌ **Content that violates third-party rights** (copyrighted materials you don't own)
- ❌ **Illegal or stolen data** (e.g., stolen intellectual property)
- ❌ **Personally Identifiable Information (PII) of employees** without proper authorization
- ❌ **Sensitive personal data** (health records, financial data) unless necessary for ISO compliance and you have proper consent

### 3.5 Misuse of AI-Generated Recommendations
You may NOT:

- ❌ Use AI-generated recommendations to create **fraudulent or misleading compliance documentation**
- ❌ Submit our gap analysis reports to auditors **as if they were official audit reports**
- ❌ Rely solely on our recommendations **without independent verification by qualified personnel**
- ❌ Submit confidential data belonging to **other organizations** without their authorization
- ❌ Attempt to manipulate our analysis by providing false or incomplete information

### 3.6 Misrepresentation
You may NOT:

- ❌ **Falsely claim ISO certification or compliance** based solely on AuditsReady recommendations
- ❌ Represent that AuditsReady **guarantees certification success**
- ❌ Misrepresent your relationship with AuditsReady (no partnerships, endorsements, or affiliations)
- ❌ Use our name, logo, or trademarks without written permission
- ❌ Claim our reports are **official audits** (we are consultants, not certification bodies)

## 4. RESPONSIBLE USE OF AI RECOMMENDATIONS

### 4.1 Verification Required
You acknowledge that AI-generated recommendations in our gap analysis reports may contain:
- Errors or omissions
- Recommendations not applicable to your specific manufacturing process or industry
- Outdated information if ISO 9001 standards change

**You must independently verify all recommendations before implementing them for compliance purposes.**

### 4.2 Human Oversight
Our AI-powered analysis is designed to **assist** your compliance efforts, not replace qualified personnel. You remain responsible for:
- Final compliance decisions
- Ensuring documentation accuracy before submitting to auditors
- Meeting all ISO 9001:2015 requirements
- Validating our recommendations with qualified ISO experts or consultants

### 4.3 Document Quality
You are responsible for:
- Submitting accurate, complete, and current documentation for analysis
- Not providing misleading or falsified documents
- Ensuring document quality for meaningful gap analysis
- Disclosing any unique circumstances that may affect our recommendations

## EXAMPLES OF VIOLATIONS

**Scenario 1: Reselling Deliverables ❌**
A manufacturing consultant pays for gap analysis for one client, then resells the report and recommendations to 5 other similar manufacturers without our permission.
**Violation:** Reselling deliverables, competitive use, unauthorized redistribution

**Scenario 2: Fraudulent Compliance ❌**
A customer receives our gap analysis report, submits it to their certification body as if it were an official audit, and falsely represents they have addressed all gaps.
**Violation:** Misuse of deliverables, misrepresentation to certification body, potential fraud

**Scenario 3: Stolen Documents ❌**
A customer submits confidential SOPs stolen from a competitor to compare their compliance levels and gain competitive advantage.
**Violation:** Use of stolen data, third-party IP infringement, unauthorized document submission

**Scenario 4: Competitive Reverse-Engineering ❌**
An ISO consulting firm purchases gap analysis services to reverse-engineer our AI methodology, report structure, and recommendations framework to build their own tool.
**Violation:** Competitive use, IP theft, building competing product

**Scenario 5: Legitimate Use ✅**
A manufacturer submits their own company's SOPs and quality documents, receives gap analysis report, reviews recommendations with their quality manager, implements changes, and prepares for ISO 9001 certification.
**Allowed:** Proper use of consulting services with human oversight and implementation

**Scenario 6: Legitimate Sharing ✅**
A customer shares our gap analysis report internally with their quality team, production managers, and CEO to plan ISO 9001 implementation.
**Allowed:** Internal sharing within the customer's organization
