## Tips for Success

### 1. **Use Consistent Information**
- Same business name, address, phone (NAP) across all directories
- Helps with SEO and credibility

### 2. **Upload High-Quality Assets**
- Logo (PNG with transparent background)
- Screenshots of your platform (when available)
- Professional headshot or team photo

### 3. **Complete Profiles 100%**
- More complete profiles rank higher in directory searches
- Fill out every optional field

### 4. **Monitor for Approval**
- Some directories require manual review (1-7 days)
- Check email for approval notifications
- Update tracker when live

### 5. **Check Backlinks**
- After 1-2 weeks, use tool like Ahrefs or Moz to verify backlink is live
- Or manually visit directory and search for your listing

## Questions or Issues?

If a directory:
- Requires payment → Skip for now unless high ROI (ASQ is worth considering)
- Rejects your listing → Note in tracker, try again in 3 months
- Takes too long → Move to next, come back later
- Is unclear → Email their support for guidance
